Policies

Financing

All payments must be paid up front when ordering a good or service, we do not do payments on time. We accept all credit and debit cards as well as cash. No checks.

Return Policy

For returns made after 60 days, or if you do not have a receipt, you will be issued an in-store only merchandise credit for the listed store price or allowed to exchange the product for something else. If exchanging for a different item, you will receive credit for the amount paid as listed on your Store Return Receipt.If you are not 100% satisfied with your purchase, you can return it to one of our retail store locations. Items purchased in-store can be returned with a receipt within 60 days for a full refund in the original form of tender. Returns without a receipt but with valid identification after the 60 days, are eligible for a merchandise credit or an exchange equal to the lowest price at which the returned products were sold. Exclusions to this policy include: grooming tools; filters or pumps; pet medication or vitamins; dog collars and leashes; electronics; and pest control treatments. These products require a valid Petco receipt for any exchange or merchandise credit. In store services (such as animal boarding) & the purchase of any saltwater fish are nonrefundable.

Delivery

We do not do any deliveries.
